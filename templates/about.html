A brief overview of our group


We are a group that is part of the young enterprise scheme in 2021/2022. We are located in the United Kingdom and attend Nottingham High School. We aim to make money through products and services. The point of this website is so that we can achieve greater community engagement and feedback on our ideas as well as being a platform where we can supply information about our products and services.
